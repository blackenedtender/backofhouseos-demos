## One-line summary

Revenue Desk turns fragmented RFP and opportunity intake into structured, reviewable revenue workflow records.

## Problem

Revenue workflows break when opportunity inputs are scattered across inboxes, decks, documents, pricing sheets, and people's memory.

## System

Revenue Desk creates a governed intake layer that captures required fields, creates structured records, supports review, and makes prior work reusable.

## Proof Loop

RFP intake -> structured record -> checklist review -> generated internal brief -> approval -> reusable opportunity history.

## What It Proves

Revenue Desk proves the BackOfHouseOS pattern in an enterprise revenue workflow context.

## Public Safety

Demo uses synthetic data only.
