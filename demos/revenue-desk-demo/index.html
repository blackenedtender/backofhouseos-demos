Revenue Desk — Opportunity Workflow System

Revenue Desk turns fragmented RFP and opportunity intake into structured, reviewable revenue workflow records.

Public-safe demo
What it is

A workflow layer for revenue teams

  • Ingests RFPs and opportunity requests as sample source inputs.
  • Drafts structured summaries, key asks, owners, dates, and deliverables.
  • Links briefs, reusable language, checklists, and response history.
  • Presents a clean operating view for sales, strategy, proposal, and GTM teams.
What it achieves

Cleaner response work

  • Faster time to first structured brief.
  • Clear ownership and review status for every opportunity.
  • Reusable workflow history instead of repeated searching.
  • Human-reviewed records before response action.
